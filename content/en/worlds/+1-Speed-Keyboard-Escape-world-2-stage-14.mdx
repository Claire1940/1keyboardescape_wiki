<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough for World 2 Stage 14">

Clearing World 2 Stage 14 requires memorizing the exact sequence of jumps and waiting for specific visual cues. Follow this step-by-step progression route to safely reach the next checkpoint.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Speed at the Safe Zone Gate">
    Before stepping onto the active hazard area, walk in place or stand near the gate to build your passive Speed. Ensure your best Trail and Aura are equipped to maximize your speed per step.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Time the Chocolate Tsunami Wave">
    Stand at the edge of the starting platform. Watch the chocolate wave's movement pattern. Unlike Stage 6 where you wait for the wave to pass, in Stage 14 you must run immediately as the faster wave appears and begins to recede.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Controlled Keyboard Jumps">
    Sprint across the initial row of letter keys. Do not jump continuously; instead, run straight through the flat sections and use single, well-timed jumps only when crossing the gaps between the split keyboard keycaps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Clear the Final Chase Gap">
    As you reach the end of the platform chain, the chase pressure increases. Hold forward steadily, do not stop to look back, and make a final long jump toward the yellow win pad to secure your progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
If you find yourself overshooting the keyboard keys due to extreme speed, try toggling your run speed or taking shorter, manual steps instead of holding down the sprint button continuously.
</Callout>
