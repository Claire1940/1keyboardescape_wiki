The most notorious obstacle in the second world is **+1 Speed Keyboard Escape world 2 stage 9**, commonly referred to by the community as the "Teddy Maze." Success in this stage is less about raw speed and more about route memorization. Because the camera angles can be deceptive, following a pre-planned path is the only way to ensure a first-try victory.
