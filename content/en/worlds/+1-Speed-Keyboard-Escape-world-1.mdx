<Section icon={Map} title="Navigating the Hazards of +1 Speed Keyboard Escape World 1">

World 1 introduces players to the fundamental obstacles that define the game. While the early stages are simple platforming challenges, later stages require precise timing and route memorization. One wrong step can send your character tumbling into the void, resetting your progress to the last checkpoint.

Stage 4 presents the first real challenge with small gap jumps. At low speeds, players must time their jumps perfectly. However, once you reach approximately 100 speed, you can run straight across these gaps without jumping. Stage 5 features a countdown bridge that drops players if they cross too early. The key to surviving this hazard is waiting for the on-screen wait signal to disappear before stepping onto the bridge.

Stages 6 and 14 introduce the chocolate tsunami, a massive wave that sweeps across the course. On Stage 6, wait until the wave passes your immediate position before dashing forward. On Stage 14, the tsunami moves much faster, requiring you to run the moment the wave appears on the horizon.

<Callout type="warning" title="Tsunami Hazards">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Treadmill Training and Speed Farming Strategies">

Farming speed manually by running around the lobby or repeating stages can be time-consuming. To optimize your progression, utilize the training treadmills located at the spawn point. These treadmills allow your character to run in place, generating speed passively without the risk of falling off platforms.

The free Chocolate treadmill offers a x1 multiplier, matching your base speed generation. While this is sufficient for the early game, premium treadmills provide massive multipliers that accelerate your progression. The Diamond treadmill, priced at 259 Robux, offers a x9 multiplier and represents the best value for regular players. For players looking to maximize their speed gains, the Admin treadmill offers a x100 multiplier, allowing you to reach rebirth requirements in minutes.

To maximize your farming efficiency, combine treadmills with active multipliers from trails, auras, and rebirths. Stacking these bonuses creates a compounding effect, significantly reducing the time required to unlock later stages.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Rebirth Milestones and Trail Progression">

Rebirthing is the primary progression system in the game. When you perform a rebirth, your current speed level resets to zero, but you receive a permanent speed multiplier. This multiplier applies to all future speed generation, allowing you to rebuild your level much faster.

The first rebirth requires Level 15 and grants a x1.5 multiplier. As you progress, the level requirements increase, but the multiplier rewards scale dramatically. By the time you reach Rebirth 10, you will require Level 200 but receive a x10 multiplier.

Wins collected from finishing stages are used to purchase Trails and Auras. Unlike speed, Wins, Trails, and Auras do not reset when you rebirth. The Blue Trail, costing 1,500 Wins, offers a x2 multiplier, while the free Medal Aura provides another x2 multiplier. Equipping these items together provides a substantial boost to your speed farming sessions.

<Callout type="success" title="Rebirth Efficiency">
Always check your rebirth button when you reach a safe zone. Rebirthing immediately when you hit the level threshold is the fastest way to scale your multipliers.
</Callout>
