Clearing the shifting keys requires a systematic approach. Follow these steps to navigate the platform pattern without falling back to the checkpoint.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Speed at the Checkpoint">
    Stand on the safe platform at the start of Stage 10. Walk in small circles to build your active speed to at least 450. Do not jump until you reach this threshold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Observe the Shifting Pattern">
    Watch the first three keyboard keys. They shift side-to-side in a three-second loop. Wait until the first key moves to its leftmost position before preparing your jump.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the First Jump Chain">
    Jump onto the first key as it reaches the left limit. Immediately leap to the second key without stopping. Stopping on moving keys ruins your jump momentum.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Time the Final Gap">
    The final gap before the safe pad is wider. Wait on the third key for a brief second, then perform a long jump while holding the forward key to land on the safe pad.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Reset Hazard">
If you fall during Step 3, you will reset at the start of Stage 10. Do not attempt to salvage a bad jump by stopping; it is better to reset and try the momentum chain again.
</Callout>
