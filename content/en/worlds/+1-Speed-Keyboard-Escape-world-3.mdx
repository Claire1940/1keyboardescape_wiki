<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step World 3 Walkthrough">

Conquering the stages in World 3 requires a mix of high raw Speed and memorized movement patterns. Follow this step-by-step progression route to complete the world.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare at the Spawn Lobby">
    Before teleporting to World 3, spend 10 minutes AFK training on the best Treadmill you can access. If you are a free-to-play player, use the free Chocolate Treadmill. If you have Robux, the Diamond (x9) or Candy (x25) Treadmills offer the best progression value.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Floating Keycaps">
    The initial stages of World 3 feature floating, spaced-out keyboard keys. Do not hold down your sprint key constantly. At speeds over 1,500, normal jumps will overshoot the platforms. Tap your forward key lightly to maintain control.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Bypass the Speed Gates">
    Mid-way through World 3, you will encounter red laser Speed Gates. These gates require a minimum Speed level to pass through safely. If your Speed is too low, the gate will instantly vaporize your character, sending you back to the start of the section.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Solve the Chocolate Maze">
    Unlike World 1 and 2 mazes, the World 3 maze features shifting walls and falling hazards. Keep your camera zoomed out to a third-person perspective to spot falling chocolate blocks before they hit you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Leap of Faith">
    The final stage of World 3 requires a massive jump across a wide void onto the golden finish pad. You must have at least 5,000+ Speed to clear this gap. Activate your 2x Speed Boost from the shop (obtained by watching a short ad) right before making the run.
  </Step>
</Steps>
