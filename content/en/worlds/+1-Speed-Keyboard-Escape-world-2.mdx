<Section icon={Map} title="+1 Speed Keyboard Escape World 2 Stage Guide">

Navigating the stages in **+1 Speed Keyboard Escape world 2** requires a mix of speed control, timing, and route memorization. Unlike World 1, where you can often sprint blindly, World 2 introduces active hazards like closing gates, falling obstacles, wind gusts, and chasing bosses. 

You must learn to adjust your momentum. Having too much speed can make short jumps difficult to control, while having too little speed will prevent you from clearing the wider gaps. The key to consistency is learning the specific patterns of each major hazard stage. 

<Callout type="tip" title="Momentum Control">
If you find yourself overshooting platform landings, try releasing the movement keys briefly mid-air. Controlling your forward momentum is just as important as building maximum speed.
</Callout>
