<Section icon={Target} title="Rebirth Strategy and Multipliers">

Rebirthing is the main progression loop in the game. When you rebirth, your current speed level resets to zero, but you receive a permanent multiplier that increases all future speed gains. Knowing when to rebirth is key to optimizing your progression route.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="First Rebirth Milestone">
    Reach level 15 to unlock your first rebirth. This grants a 1.5x permanent multiplier. Do this immediately to start scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Early Rebirth Stacking">
    Repeat the process up to Rebirth 5 (requires level 75, grants 3.5x multiplier). Use the lobby buttons to rebuild speed quickly after resets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mid-Game Breakpoint">
    Aim for Rebirth 10 (requires level 200). This unlocks a massive 10x multiplier, making treadmill farming highly effective.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Late-Game Scaling">
    Push to Rebirth 15 (requires level 380, grants 1M multiplier) and Rebirth 20 (requires level 600, grants 100B multiplier) to dominate the leaderboards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Rebirthing does not reset your Wins, Trails, or Auras. Therefore, always ensure you have the best possible gear equipped before resetting your level so you can rebuild your speed in seconds.

<Callout type="info" title="Rebirth Level Scaling">
Each subsequent rebirth requires a higher level threshold. Do not delay rebirthing once you reach the target level, as farming beyond the threshold yields diminishing returns.
</Callout>
