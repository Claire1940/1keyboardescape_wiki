<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Speed Farming and Multiplier Strategies">

To progress efficiently, you need to understand how speed stacks. You aren't limited to just walking; the game provides several "Number Buttons" in the lobby. Standing on these buttons increases your speed per step based on how many Wins you have spent. This is the most consistent way to build a foundation before you start buying gear.

| Farming Method | Requirement | Speed Gain per Step | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Walking** | None | +1 Speed | Default movement for new players |
| **Starter Button** | 3 Wins | +2 Speed | Your first upgrade after stage 1 |
| **Pro Button** | 100 Wins | +25 Speed | Stable farming for World 1 mid-stages |
| **Elite Button** | 2,500 Wins | +100 Speed | Essential for reaching Rebirth 5 |
| **Master Button** | 50,000 Wins | +500 Speed | Late-game speed scaling for World 2 |

Beyond buttons, you should focus on stacking different types of multipliers. The game calculates your speed as: `(Base Step + Button Bonus) x Rebirth Multiplier x Trail Multiplier x Aura Multiplier`. By diversifying where your bonuses come from, you avoid hitting a "progression wall" where upgrades become too expensive.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Stage Walkthrough and Hazard Guide">

The obby stages in **+1 Speed Keyboard Escape** are designed to punish players who move too fast without control. While high speed is necessary for long jumps, it can make landing on small keyboard keys difficult. World 1 is relatively forgiving, but World 2 introduces hazards that can reset your progress to the last safe zone.

| Hazard Type | Location | Best Strategy | Reset Risk |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chocolate Tsunami** | World 1, Stage 6 | Wait for the wave to pass, then sprint | High |
| **Timing Gates** | World 2, Stage 2 | Start moving exactly when the door opens | Moderate |
| **Teddy Boss Maze** | World 2, Stage 9 | Follow: Left, Right, Right, Right, Left | Extreme |
| **Falling Spiders** | World 2, Stage 7 | Keep moving; do not stop to aim jumps | Moderate |
| **Wind Fans** | World 2, Stage 8 | Use steady speed; avoid jumping in gusts | High |

For the **Teddy Maze** (Stage 9), the boss will chase you. If you hesitate, you will be reset. Memorizing the pattern (L-R-R-R-L) is the only way to clear it consistently. For the **Tsunami** stages, timing is more important than speed. If you jump into the wave, you will be knocked back regardless of your level.

<Callout type="warning" title="Checkpoint Safety">
Yellow pads are your checkpoints. Always ensure you see the "Saved" notification or hear the sound effect before moving to the next obstacle. If you fall before touching the next pad, you will return to the previous one.
</Callout>
