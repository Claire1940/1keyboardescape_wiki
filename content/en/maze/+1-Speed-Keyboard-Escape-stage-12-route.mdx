*   **Sprinting Blindly at High Speeds**: If your speed is over 250, you might overshoot the narrow corridors and get stuck on the walls, allowing the boss to catch up. Practice controlled, steady turning rather than holding down forward blindly.

*   **Taking the Left Path at the Start**: The left side of the maze is a dead end. Always stick to the right-hand wall immediately upon entering.
