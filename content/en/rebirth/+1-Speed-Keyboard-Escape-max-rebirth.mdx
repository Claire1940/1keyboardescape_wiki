<Section icon={Map} title="Stage Walkthrough and Route Tips">

Clearing stages rewards you with wins, which are required to buy the buttons, trails, and auras needed for the **+1 Speed Keyboard Escape max rebirth** tiers. While speed helps you cross gaps, timing and route memory are what prevent costly resets.

World 1 and World 2 feature specific hazards that require controlled movements. Sprinting blindly at maximum speed will often cause you to overshoot small platforms or run directly into hazards.

<Callout type="warning" title="Reset Risk">
One wrong jump sends you back to the start of the stage chain. Take your time on timing-based obstacles like bridges and waves.
</Callout>

### World 1 Key Stages
- **Stage 4 (Parkour)**: Skip early jumps if you have 100+ speed. At extremely high speeds, run straight through without jumping to maintain control.
- **Stage 5 (Bridge)**: Wait for the on-screen signal before crossing. Rushing immediately when the countdown starts reduces your safe crossing window.
- **Stage 6 & 14 (Tsunami)**: For Stage 6, wait for the wave to pass right in front of you before moving. For Stage 14, run immediately when the fast wave appears.
- **Stage 11 (Launcher)**: Hold forward and do not jump. The launcher will automatically propel you across the gap, saving time.
- **Stage 12 (Keyboard Maze)**: Take the first right, follow the path, turn right at the first intersection, then take the next right to exit.

### World 2 Key Stages
- **Stage 2 (Gates)**: Start moving immediately after the first gate opens to clear the sequence before they shut.
- **Stage 7 & 8 (Hazards)**: Keep moving forward steadily. Spiders and fan gusts are timed to hit players who hesitate.
- **Stage 9 (Teddy Maze)**: Follow the exact pattern: **Left → Right → Right → Right → Left** to escape the chasing boss.
- **Stage 15 (Final Stage)**: Ensure you are level 550+ for optimal jump distance. Clearing this stage rewards a massive +200,000,000 wins.
