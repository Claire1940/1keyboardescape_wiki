<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Medal Quest Walkthrough">

Completing the quest takes less than five minutes. The process requires jumping between the Medal application and Roblox, so follow these exact steps to ensure the system registers your progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Download and Install Medal">
    Go to the official **Medal.tv** website on your PC or download the Medal app on your mobile device. Create a free account or log in using your Discord credentials. Keep the app running in the background.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Link Your Roblox Account">
    Open User Settings (the gear icon) in the Medal app. Navigate to **Connected Accounts** (or Connections). Find Roblox, click **Connect**, log into your active Roblox account, and authorize the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Record a Gameplay Clip">
    Launch the official **+1 Speed Keyboard Escape** on Roblox using your linked account. Play the game naturally for at least 15 to 30 seconds. On PC, press **F8** to save a gameplay clip. On mobile, use the floating Medal widget to capture your clip.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upload and Tag Your Clip">
    Return to the Medal app and open your library. Select your new clip and prepare to post it. You must set the visibility to **Everyone (Public)**. Search for and select **Roblox** as the main game, and tag **+1 Speed Keyboard Escape** as the subgame. Click **Post**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Claim Your Medal Aura">
    Once the clip finishes uploading, return to the Roblox game lobby. Open the **Medal Quest** menu on your screen. The system will verify your public upload and show a checkmark. Press **Claim**, open your inventory, navigate to Auras, and equip your new **Medal Aura**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Crucial Upload Settings">
Many players fail to receive their reward because they miss two critical settings during Step 4:
1. **Visibility**: Your clip *must* be public. If it is set to private, the verification system cannot see it.
2. **Subgame Tagging**: Ensure you tag the official game version (often listed with "World 3" or "Candy & Chocolate" in the title) rather than a copycat experience.
</Callout>
