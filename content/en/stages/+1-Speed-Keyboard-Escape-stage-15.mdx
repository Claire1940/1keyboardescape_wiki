To clear **+1 Speed Keyboard Escape stage 15** consistently, you need to break the course down into three distinct phases. Each phase requires a different tactical approach to avoid a total reset.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Entrance Gates">
    The stage begins with a series of timed gates. Wait for the first gate to fully retract before sprinting. Do not try to "thread the needle" on the first half-second of the animation, as the hitbox is larger than the visual model.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Spiral Stairs">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Chase Sequence">
    The final stretch involves a chase mechanic similar to the Teddy Maze in Stage 9. A hazard will follow you from behind. Navigate the Left → Right → Right zig-zag pattern on the keyboard keys without stopping to reach the final yellow pad.
  </Step>
</Steps>
