Follow these exact steps to navigate the hazards of the World 1 finale and secure your first major win payout.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your Speed Level">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Two-Ramp Jump">
    When running up the chocolate ramps, hold down your jump button continuously. Do not release the button; spamming your jumps across the ramps prevents your speed from decreasing and ensures you clear the gap.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stay Centered in the Ball Lane">
    Move into the narrow straightaway. Keep your character positioned perfectly in the middle of the two lines. Touching either boundary line will cause the large rolling balls to clip your character, knocking you off the stage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Time the Gate and Stay Left">
    Approach the final door and halt your character on the far left side. Wait patiently for the door to begin opening. The moment it moves, sprint directly through the center of the opening to avoid the hazard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Use the Left-Side Trick">
Staying on the left side of the door prevents the obstacle from clipping your character's hitbox as the door slides open. Never try to squeeze through early; wait for the animation to start.
</Callout>
