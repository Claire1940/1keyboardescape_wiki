<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Initial Turn">
    Step into the maze to trigger your 5-second head start. Run straight in and **immediately take the first right turn**. Do not wait for the boss to spawn before making this move.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Corridor Loop">
    Follow the linear corridor as it bends naturally. You will quickly make a **left turn**, followed by another **left turn**. Keep your momentum forward and avoid bumping into the chocolate walls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Three-Way Intersection">
    You will reach a three-way intersection. **Turn right** here. Keep running straight down this corridor until you see the next opening on your left.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Mid-Maze Zig-Zag">
    Take a **left turn**, followed immediately by a **right turn**, and then another **right turn**. This sequence bypasses the dead ends where the boss typically traps players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Safe Zone Sprint">
    Make one last **left turn**. The corridor opens up directly to the yellow safe zone pad in front of Stage 13. Jump onto the pad immediately to secure your progress and bank your Wins.
  </Step>
</Steps>
